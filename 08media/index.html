Artist Highlight

  • Taylor Swift

After transitioning from country music to a more pop-oriented sound, Taylor Swift has taken the world by storm with her release of albums such as 1989, Reputation, and Midnights along side songs such as Blank Space, Look What You Made Me Do, and Anti-Hero

Song of the Month

  • Bye Bye Bye (2000)

After forming as a group in the 1990's, NSYNC is one of the most recognizable boybands to boot. Their song Bye Bye Bye crushed the charts and solidified their position in pop music at the time.
